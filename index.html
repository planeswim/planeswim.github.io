Travel to Diamond Bar

Written by Tyler Huey and Anzhi Chen

Location fo Diamond Bar

Located in Southern California:
Part of the Los Angeles county, and it borders Orange County and San Bernardino County
Diamond Bar is about 40 miles away from Los Angeles International Airport or 30-35 miles from Downtown LA. The city is right off of the 57 and 60 interchange.

Overall Highlights of Diamond Bar

  • A variety of Great Schools(all levels)
  • Many recreations activities such as sports, senior services, and more
  • Opportunity to build or start business
  • Great amount of Servies including public service alerts, public transportation, public works, and more

Reasons to Visit Diamond Bar

  • Many Fun Parks all over the city with unique hiking trails
  • Wonderful Views of Nature
  • Several Community Centers(avaible to rent for hosting events)
  • Places to Stay: Nice Hotels
  • Community Events
